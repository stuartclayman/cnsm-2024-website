Call for Posters

CNSM 2024 will include poster sessions to provide a venue for researchers, young professionals, and MSc. and PhD. students to exchange ideas, and share recent results, work in progress, and experiences in network and service management. CNSM 2024 poster track covers all aspects of the management of networks and services, pervasive systems, enterprises, and cloud computing environments. In particular, CNSM 2024 will focus on AI-Powered Network and Service Management for Tomorrow's Digital World.

Posters are solicited for any of the research topics relevant to CNSM and can be any (but not limited to) of the following:

  • Upcoming research challenges, future directions, and works in progress;
  • Preliminary results of novel ideas;
  • Research questions and solution ideas as part of PhD. and MSc.thesis;
  • Academic and industry collaboration initiatives

Submissions should be up to 5 pages long (including references), follow the IEEE conference 2-column template, and have a minimum font size of 10 points. Submissions will be evaluated mainly on their potential to stimulate interesting discussions, exchange of ideas, and promote collaborations. At least one author of each accepted poster paper is required to register to CNSM conference and present the poster at the scheduled poster session. A best poster award will be presented at the CNSM 2024 closing ceremony.

Posters can be submitted via EDAS using this link: https://edas.info/N32230

Important Dates

Submission deadline: 5 August 2024
Notification of acceptance: 3 September 2024
Camera-ready: 13 September 2023
