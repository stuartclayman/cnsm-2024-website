CNSM 2024 Registration

Each paper, including Workshop, Poster, and Demo papers, must be covered with an Author registration.

Author registration for full/short/poster/demo papers, mini-conference papers, and workshop papers is due on September 27, 2024, except for Workshop Papers Authors for which the registration deadline is October 7, 2024.

The advanced registration for participants ends on October 7, 2024.

Late Registration starts on October 8, 2024.

For any request or issue regarding Registration, please contact the Registration / Publication Chair (cnsm2024-publication@edas.info).

Author Registration and Attendance Policy

  • Every paper accepted at CNSM 2024, and the workshops must have an associated registration at the Author rate.
  • For each paper – this applies for all full, short, poster, and demo papers of CNSM 2024, mini conference and workshops included – at least one co-author must be registered as author (Author rate) to secure the paper’s publication.
  • All presenters must be registered either at Author rate, or as Participant if the paper is already covered with another Author registration.
  • The registration of a single co-author as author (Author rate) will secure the publication of up to 2 papers from the same co-author.
  • All papers have to be presented by their co-authors. CNSM 2024 reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference, if the paper presentation condition is not met.
  • All the Registration Fees (rates) given in the tables below are in US Dollars and include 21% VAT.
